Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how QSalon collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: November 26, 2025

QSalon is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application (the "App") and our services.

01

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. The types of information we collect include:

Personal Information

When you register, we may ask for personal details such as your name, email address, and phone number (for OTP verification).

Location Data

We collect your precise or approximate location data to display salons in your vicinity and calculate distances to service providers.

Salon Data

If you are a salon owner/staff, we collect business details, service menus, and staff information.

Device Information

We may collect information about your mobile device, including the hardware model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

02

App Permissions

To provide specific features, QSalon may request access to:

Location (GPS/Network)

Used to identify your current location and show the "Nearby Salons" feature on the map or home screen.

Camera & Gallery

To allow you to upload profile photos, salon logos, or service images.

Internet

To connect to our servers to sync bookings and data.

Phone/SMS

To facilitate OTP authentication for secure login.

03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

Account Management

Create and manage your account securely.

Booking Services

Facilitate salon bookings and appointments.

Location-Based Services

Show you the closest salons and relevant search results.

Notifications

Send important notifications regarding bookings or app updates.

04

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

05

Third-Party Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services (e.g., payment gateways) that are not owned or controlled by QSalon. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of such third parties.

06

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

07

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
